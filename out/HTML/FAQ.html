Frequently Asked Questions

What features do users have access to?

+ -

Users can make their own accounts being able to view their credit and debit balances, able to recieve ledgers, etc.

What features do the administrator have access to?

+ -

Administrators are able to activate and deactivate the user and manager accounts along with the ability to create users and assign roles.

What are the event logs for?

+ -

Its a list of events that has occured within an account in chronological order and it helps keep track of the activities of our users.
